Anokha Tech Fest, organized by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, is an extraordinary celebration of technology, innovation, and creativity. This annual extravaganza brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals from across the nation, providing them with a unique platform to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and collaborate on cutting-edge projects. Anokha Tech Fest Amrita has gained recognition for its grandeur, diversity, and commitment to fostering innovation. This essay aims to delve into the essence of Anokha Tech Fest Amrita and its impact on the technological landscape. One of the highlights of Anokha Tech Fest Amrita is the opportunity it provides for participants to engage with industry experts, researchers, and renowned professionals.







Anokha gave me an oppurtunity to perform on stage. The second day of the Anokha tech fest was the NATYA NIGHT, also knows as the dance night. Performed, by the university's dance club 'Natya Sudha', the fest had a night enjoyed by everyone. I am in the Jhalak team of Natyasudha club. We gave a wonderful performance on 5 different songs. We were also the first ones to perform on stage. We had performed on 5 different bollywood songs from different movies. The team leader had always supported all of us and gave all her time in the performances. Multiple groups had performed that day. There were around 10 groups that performed on stage that day. From which there were around 6 girls groups and 3 boy groups and one which had both boys and girls. All these performance were done by the dance club of the university. Dance styles such as classical, bollywood, contemporary, jazz and many more were performed.







The auditorium was filled with cheers and music all around. Viewers had started dancing. We loved the fact that people loved our performance. We loved their reactions, as their reaction made us enjoy more. I would like to perform more on stage. Everyone enjoyed that day. It was one the of the most memorable days of my college life.
