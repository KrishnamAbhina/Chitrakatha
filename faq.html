FAQ'S

What is CHITRA KATHA?

A platform to post and read all the latest happenings in the campus. Made by the students its a place to share your success stories , achievements , and even inspiring moments from your life. Want to know about your seniors or the alumni's ? Come and know about their placement stats , internship details etc. Not just academics this platform lets you know about sports , culturals , and other events happening at Amrita.

How to post in CHITRA KATHA?

When you log in to CHITRA KATHA you will see the post button on top. Click on it and select your wanted section to post in. After that a text box will appear for you to write. Pictures , videos etc can be attached. Rememeber your roll no is being noted while posting. Any discrepency inthe data posted , strict actions will be taken accordingly.

How to register to CHITRA KATHA?

For registering to our website you will need your AMRITA official Outlook ID and password. Once that is verified you can enter your own username ( preferably roll no. ) and a password.

What are the different fields to post under?

CHITRA KATHA provides an ample of options mainly :-
i) ACADEMICS
ii) CO-CURRICULAR
iii) INSPIRON (Placements , Internships , Alumni Network)
iv) OTHERS
which covers almost every required field a student would want to write about.
