ABOUT

Amrita's POST is an ideal medium for the faculty members and also the students to share their ideas, information and current developments in their research fields of interests related to chemical science.
Articles shedding lights on the chemistry behind our quotidian life practices and processes will also have place along with the popular chemical science articles, stories about chemists and their discoveries and innovations.
Quiz- type articles related to the particular fields of chemical science are also entertained. ‘POST’ is also a great forum for young science writers to shape their writing skills, work with an editorial team, build up confidence and grow their portfolios.

Amrita POST aims to attract both the scholars and seekers of all all departments equally and appreciate the readership of general public.
