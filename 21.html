Edition 1 - July 2018 [Volume 1, Issue 1]


It is with great pleasure that we are presenting the very first issue of our new e-magazine ''Chitra Katha' - a quarterly chemical science magazine published by the Department of Chemistry, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri.

This inaugural issue contains four main articles from our faculty members, all the four articles cover four very different aspects of chemistry and evidence the multifaceted nature of chemistry and versatility in its applications. We also have a set of ‘chemistry activity bits’ collected by our masters’ student, Ms. Sweta Anantharaman to help boost general chemistry knowledge and keep the reader entertained.

We hope that, this issue will attract the readership of scholars and the common public alike and help to popularize chemistry reading and writing habit among all Amritians and others.

Edition 3 - January 2019 [Volume 1, Issue 3]

Dear Readers,

We are pleased to present the third edition of our e-magazine, Rasatantra.

We are happy to see the growing interest on our e-magazine, equally by the contributors and readers.

This edition contains four articles, related to various facets of Chemistry, coming from different parts of the world. Renowned Nano-chemists, Prof. Oxana V. Kharissova and Prof. Boris I. Kharissov from Mexico, have contributed an article about the ‘Solubilization and Dispersion of Carbon Nanotubes and Other Carbon Allotropes.’ Dr. Prasenjit Saha from National Tobacco Testing Laboratories Guwahati, has written an article for us, about the ‘Chemistry of Tobacco and its health impacts’. Along with these, we have an article from Dr. Keyan Bennaceur and his group from Department of Physics, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri, shedding some lights on their ongoing research project, Development of portable spectrophotometer for milk quality analysis. The last article we have here, is by Dr. Sharma S.R.K.C. Yamijala from University of California, Riverside, which talks about doing ‘chemistry with computers’.

We hope you will like all these articles and get benefited by the shared information.
